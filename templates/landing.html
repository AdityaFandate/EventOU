Platform Features
Everything you need to
run a safe event.

From QR-based check-ins to zone monitoring, food coupons to real-time alerts — one dashboard for every role.

📡

Real-Time Crowd Monitoring

Track live occupancy across events, zones, and gates. Automatic warnings when crowd levels approach safe thresholds. Block entry with one click when critical levels are hit.

📲

QR Pass System

Students get digital passes with unique QR codes. Volunteers scan instantly at gates — contactless and foolproof.

🍱

Food Coupon Manager

Issue digital food coupons, track redemptions live, and prevent fraud with one-time QR validation at canteen counters.

🏟️

Zone & Gate Analytics

Divide large venues into zones, assign gates, and monitor net flow in real time with entry/exit counters per gate.

📣

Instant Announcements

Push global or event-specific announcements to attendees — schedule changes, safety alerts, or general info.

🕐

Time-Slot Booking

Manage crowd flow by letting students book entry time slots — reduce entry surges and keep queues short.
